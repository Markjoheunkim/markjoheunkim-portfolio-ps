ST. JOSEPH COLLEGE - JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL - OLONGAPO CITY INC.

2017-2021


St. Joseph College - Junior High School in Olongapo City is a private institution committed to providing a well-rounded education rooted in strong academic and moral values. The school offers a nurturing environment that encourages students to excel academically, develop critical thinking skills, and grow into responsible individuals. Known for its dedicated faculty and holistic approach to education, St. Joseph College aims to prepare students for future challenges and opportunities.


ST. JOSEPH COLLEGE - SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL - OLONGAPO CITY INC.

2021-2023


St. Joseph College - Senior High School in Olongapo City is a private institution dedicated to providing quality education with a focus on academic excellence and character development. The school offers various strands under the Academic Track, including STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics), ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management), HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences), as well as TVL (Technical-Vocational-Livelihood) and ICT (Information and Communications Technology). These programs are designed to prepare students for higher education, employment, and other future opportunities, all within a supportive and nurturing environment.


GORDON COLLEGE

2023-ONGOING


Gordon College is a public educational institution located in Olongapo City, Philippines, offering a range of academic programs, including undergraduate and graduate degrees. Known for its commitment to quality education, the college provides a supportive learning environment with modern facilities, experienced faculty, and a focus on practical skills and community engagement. It aims to equip students with the knowledge and competencies needed for their professional careers and personal growth.
